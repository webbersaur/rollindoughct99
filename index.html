Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking our pizza trailer

How much does pizza truck catering cost in Connecticut?

Our all-inclusive pizza trailer catering packages start at $1,400 for up to 50 guests, $1,700 for 50–75 guests, and $2,000 for 75–100 guests. Every package includes 2 salads, all-you-can-eat wood fired pizza, and a choice of dessert (gelato, Italian ice, or cannoli). Tax is included in pricing.

What areas does Rollin Dough serve in Connecticut?

We're based in Guilford, CT and serve all of Connecticut. We frequently cater events in Guilford, Madison, Branford, New Haven, Hamden, North Haven, Milford, Old Saybrook, Middletown, Wallingford, Cheshire, and surrounding towns along the shoreline and beyond.

What is included in a Rollin Dough catering package?

Every package includes 2 fresh salads to start (Caesar and Mixed Greens), all-you-can-eat wood fired apizza with your choice of pies from our full menu, and a dessert course — gelato, Italian ice, or cannoli. Tax is included. Gluten free options are also available.

What types of events does Rollin Dough cater?

Our mobile pizza truck caters weddings, graduation parties, corporate events, birthday pizza parties, block parties, fundraisers, and any private event across Connecticut. Whether it's a backyard pizza party or a 100-guest wedding, we roll up with our wood fired oven and serve fresh pies on-site.

Can I book a pizza truck for a wedding in Connecticut?

Absolutely! Food truck wedding catering is one of our most popular bookings. Our wood fired pizza truck adds a unique, memorable experience to your big day. Guests love watching their pies come straight from the oven. We serve weddings across Connecticut — from shoreline venues in Guilford and Madison to inland locations in Cheshire and Wallingford.

Does Rollin Dough offer gluten free pizza?

Yes! We offer gluten free pizza options. Just let us know when you book your event and we'll make sure gluten free pies are available for your guests.

What style of pizza does Rollin Dough make?

We make authentic New Haven-style apizza, cooked in a wood fired oven. Our pies feature hand-stretched dough with a signature char, just like the legendary New Haven pizzerias. We offer both red and white pies, including signature creations like The Marco and Clams Casino.
